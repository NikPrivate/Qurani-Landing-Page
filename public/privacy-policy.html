Glosari Budaya Qurani Privacy Policy

Home

1. Introduction

Welcome to Glosari Budaya Qurani. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Web Admin Panel:
    • User Credentials: We collect login information (username, password) for registered users.
    • User Roles and Permissions: We store role and permission details to manage access levels.
  • Mobile App:
    • Public Information: The mobile app displays “perkataan” from Qurani. No personal data is collected from mobile users.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Web Admin Panel:
    • To manage user accounts and permissions.
    • To allow authorized users to add, edit, or delete “perkataan.”
    • To maintain communication and system security.
  • Mobile App:
    • To display “perkataan” details to the public.

4. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated through appropriate channels.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at arnida@usim.edu.my.
